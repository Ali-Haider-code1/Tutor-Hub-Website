Website Goals

Goal:

To provide a user-friendly platform that connects students with qualified teachers, enabling students to easily find, hire, and schedule appointments with teachers for personalized learning experiences.

Key Objectives:

  1. Connecting Students and Teachers

    Create a digital space where students can browse and connect with a variety of teachers across different subjects and expertise levels.

  2. Hiring Teachers

    Enable students to hire teachers based on their specific learning needs, whether it's for academic subjects, skill development, or any other educational purpose.

  3. Scheduling Appointments

    Facilitate the booking of appointments at convenient times for both students and teachers, ensuring flexibility in the learning process.

  4. Personalized Learning

    Support personalized learning experiences by allowing students to choose teachers who align with their learning goals and teaching styles.

  5. User-Friendly Interface

    Create an intuitive and user-friendly website interface that makes it easy for both students and teachers to navigate and use the platform.

  6. Feedback

    Allow students to leave feedback for teachers, helping others make informed choices.
